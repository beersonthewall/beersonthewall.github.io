Programmers as Athletes

November 11, 2021

What would a training program for programmers look like if you approached it like training a professional athlete? There’s a lot of money to be made as a programmer, why wouldn’t we want to design training programs to “play in the big leagues”? Or, if that style of company isn’t your dream job, maybe you find programming intellectually gratifying and just want to learn and hone your skills.
